About
Michaela

Passionate about unlocking human potential and fostering business growth, I merge expertise in consulting, psychology, and data analysis to elevate success, wellness, and innovation for your business.

As a child, I was fascinated by patterns, math, and reasoning. My enjoyment of mathematics and logic manifested throughout my academic studies. In my teen years, I also developed an interest in human psychology, leading to an in-depth study of psychology in college. Over four years, I conducted psychological studies in multiple labs, gaining firsthand experience in the entire research process: designing, piloting, and testing phases, implementing the study, collecting data, and analyzing findings, which included coding and presenting results.

My role as a research analyst at BYU and UVU provided multiple opportunities to author articles, present at professional conferences, and offer consulting services to local Utah companies. Upon graduating with a Bachelor's of Science in Psychology, I continued my work as a Data Analyst and delved into the world of technology. This is when the field of Data Science came into view. Immersing myself in Python, PowerBi, Excel, and a plethora of other technologies has expanded my capacity to serve businesses and help them thrive through harnessing the power of data science.

Avidly pursuing learning is a core value of my life. I continuously strive for innovation in my personal and professional areas, which I aim to share with others through volunteering and professional work.
